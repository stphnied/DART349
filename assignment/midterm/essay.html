Mental health awareness

Proposal: Narrative HTML on mental health issue (anxiety).

Anyone can feel an appropriate level of nervousness faced in a nerve-wracking situation. Feeling nervous for an oral presentation or for a job interview is something that most human beings can relate to. However, people with anxiety disorder often have intense worry and fear about everyday situations. The feeling of anxiety can interfere with their daily routine and cause them to stop functioning.
